Healthy wraps with only 4 ingredients

These delicious and healthy wraps just need 4 ingredients and are prepared within 5 minutes 🌯👩🏼‍🍳👨🏽‍🍳

Zutaten:

      2-3 eggs
      1/3 paprika
      Grated cheese
      1 tortilla wrap
      Salt & pepper
      Some herbs

Instructions

  1. Cut paprika in little pieces or stripes
  2. Crack the eggs and whisk them well
  3. Give eggs into frying pan at medium heat
  4. Flavour with salt, pepper and herbs
  5. Add paprika and cheese
  6. Place tortilla wrap on top and wait a minute till the wrap sticks to the cheese
  7. Flip around carefully for a crusty wrap
  8. Remove wrap from pan, roll it, and cut in the middle

There are no limits to your creativity. You can add more ingredients, like bacon, mushrooms, or tomatoes 😋

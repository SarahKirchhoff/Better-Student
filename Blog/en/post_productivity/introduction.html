Everything you need to know about me and this blog

Hello and welcome to my blog!!

My name is Sarah and I am the owner of this website, as well as of the Instagram Account „the.better_student“ and these Twitter and Pinterest Accounts.

Currently I am studying Physics in my master's with a specialisation in atmospheric physics and research. If everything is going on as planed, I am going to start with my master's thesis in April. Alongside my studies, I am working as a students assitant at the department for atmospheric research at my University.

Already one year ago, in January 2021, I decided to share my life as a physics student on Instagram. On of the reasons for that was, that I lost all my motivation in the current Uni@home situation and my productivity was as low as never before. Real quick I found new motivation and exchanged with other science communicators and study bloggers. This community got me back on track and I would say that right now I am more motivated and by for more productive than ever before.

Because of these amazing experiences, I now decided to start this blog to reach even more people. I love to see how my content is helping others to become a better version of themselfs. So, if you are a bit unmotivated right now and if you are looking for some advice, you are exactly on the right address!

I will share actionable productivty tips I learned durning my time as a physics student, as well as practical life advices. Besides that, I want to share some books with you that have helped me to become the person I am right now.

Did I spark your interest? Feel free to subscribe to my FREE newsletter.
